About The Mandir

A Lamp Lit in 1984, Still Burning Bright

OUR HISTORY

FOUR DECADES OF DEVOTION

Founded in 1984 by a small circle of devoted families, Shree Sanatan Mandir began as a humble shrine in a single room. Through decades of community seva, it has grown into a vibrant cultural and spiritual sanctuary — yet the original lamp lit on its first day continues to burn without interruption.

Every stone, every carving, every garland here has been offered by hands that believe service to others is service to the divine.

OUR VISION & MISSION

At Shree Pitambar Pith Dham, our vision is to create a sanctuary where ancient wisdom meets modern devotion — a place where every visitor feels the warmth of community and the light of spiritual growth.

Our mission is to preserve and share our sacred traditions through selfless service, educational programs, and cultural events that inspire and uplift all who walk through our doors.
